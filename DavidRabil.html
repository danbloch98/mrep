DAVID RABIL

David Rabil is the second professional golfer to join the Raymond Representation family. Rabil played an elite level of golf early on in high school from 2011-2015. At South County High School, Rabil set school records for 9,18, and 36 hole scoring and earned all-conference honors three times as well as all-region honors as a junior. He then went on to Christopher Newport University where he started off hot right away. As a freshman, Rabil earned second-team All-Conference which included three top-10 finishes that year. In his sophomore season, he became the fourth Captain to secure first-team PING All-American honor.
